Macoroni Recipe

Samiur, Ismail, Omar

Ingredients You'll Need

  • 8 Ounces of Box Elbow Macoroni
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • Ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 cups of shredded cheddar

Steps To bake Macoroni

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to boil
  2. Cook elbow Macoroni in the boiling water
    stirring occasianlly until cooked throught but firm enough to bite for 8 minutes
  3. Drain
  4. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat
  5. Stir in flour, salt
    and pepper until smooth for about 5 minutes
  6. Slowly pur milk into butter-flour mixture while continuously stirring until mixture is smooth and bubbling
  7. Add cheddar cheese to milk mixture and stir until cheese is melted for 2 to 4 minutes
  8. Fold macoroni into cheese sauce until coated
  9. Enjoy!
